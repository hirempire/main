Usage Guidelines

Logo Usage

  • Always maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use the primary blue (#3349C0) on light backgrounds
  • Use white version on dark backgrounds
  • Never distort, rotate, or modify the logo
  • Minimum size: 120px width for digital use

Color Guidelines

  • Primary blue is our main brand color
  • Use secondary blue for gradients and accents
  • Gray tones for text and subtle elements
  • Maintain WCAG AA contrast ratios
  • Light blue for backgrounds and subtle highlights

Typography Rules

  • Use Space Grotesk for all headings and titles
  • Use Manrope for body text and descriptions
  • Maintain proper hierarchy with font weights
  • Ensure readability across all devices
  • Line height should be 1.4-1.6 for body text

Brand Voice

  • Professional yet approachable
  • Innovative and forward-thinking
  • Clear and concise communication
  • Empowering and confident tone
  • Human-centered and empathetic
